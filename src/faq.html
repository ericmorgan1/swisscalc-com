What is SwissCalc?

SwissCalc is an online calculator accessible by most browsers.

Just as the Swiss Army Knife has a tool for every job, SwissCalc aims to provide a calculator for any type of calculation, from basic arithmetic, to financial analysis, to calculating tips at a restaurant.

This calculator is targeted toward mobile devices, however it also works well on desktop computers. (The calculator will respond to keyboard inputs and you can resize your browser window to simulate "rotating" your phone).

The goal of SwissCalc is to be the "go to" calculator for all mobile devices. If you have any suggestions for upgrades or improvements, please contact us at your convenience.

Can I use SwissCalc offline?

Yes! SwissCalc utilizes the "cache manifest" feature of HTML5 to enable the calculators to be used offline. As long as your browser is compatible, SwissCalc will continue to function without the need for an internet connection.

iPhone users can open SwissCalc in Safari and save the calculators to their phone. A custom icon will appear and SwissCalc will launch in full screen mode.
